Intro

I am currently working as a geospatial analyst for the Facilities Management Department at UVA. Throughout my professional career in GIS, I have worked across various industries where GIS played a critical role in daily operations. My experience includes map design, production, and publishing, telecom infrastructure design, digital geophysical mapping, and facility management in higher education.

In terms of my educational background, I hold a bachelor's degree in Geography, a bachelor's degree in GIS, and a master's degree in Environmental Science.

In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, camping, and traveling, and I love spending quality time with my family.

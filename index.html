Intro

I am currently working as a geo-spatial analyst for the Facilities Management Department at UVA. Throughout my professional career in GIS, I have worked across various industries where GIS played a critical role in daily operations. My experience includes map design, production, and publishing, telecom infrastructure design, digital geophysical mapping, and facility management in higher education.

In terms of my educational background, I hold a bachelor's degree in Geography, a bachelor's degree in GIS, and a master's degree in Environmental Science.

In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, camping, and traveling, and I love spending quality time with my family.

Work

Over my career, I have had the chance to work in a variety of industries, including facility management in higher education, digital geophysical mapping, telecom infrastructure design, and map production and publishing. In every role, database management has been an essential part of my daily work, while GIS technology has been a key tool enabling me to achieve success and make meaningful contributions.

Currently, as a geo-spatial analyst I am responsible for geo-spatial data analysis, maintaining database information, and responding to customer requests for geo-spatial solutions.

In my previous role, I was a key player in managing GIS operations for over 10 digital geophysical mapping projects, ensuring their successful completion. My contributions were critical at every stage of these projects.

Additionally, as a field data collection specialist, I was responsible for planning and scheduling daily site activities, specifically collecting pole data related to existing telecom infrastructure.

Finally, I have nine years of experience as a cartographer, which provided me with a solid foundation in mapping projects and prepared me for success in my subsequent roles.

These experiences have equipped me with the ability to translate complex datasets into actionable insights, allowing me to provide effective geo-spatial solutions and drive project success.

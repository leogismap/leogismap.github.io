Leo Feng's Professional Journey

Career Summary

Over my career, I’ve had the chance to work in a variety of industries, including facility management in higher education, digital geophysical mapping, telecom infrastructure design, and map production and publishing. In every role, database management has been an essential part of my daily work, while GIS technology has been a key tool enabling me to achieve success and make meaningful contributions.

Currently, as a geospatial analyst, I am responsible for geospatial data analysis, maintaining database information, and responding to customer requests for geospatial solutions.

In my previous role, I was a key player in managing GIS operations for over 10 digital geophysical mapping projects, ensuring their successful completion. My contributions were critical at every stage of these projects.

Additionally, as a field data collection specialist, I was responsible for planning and scheduling daily site activities, specifically collecting pole data related to existing telecom infrastructure.

Finally, I have nine years of experience as a cartographer, which provided me with a solid foundation in mapping projects and prepared me for success in my subsequent roles.

These experiences have equipped me with the ability to translate complex datasets into actionable insights, allowing me to provide effective geospatial solutions and drive project success.
